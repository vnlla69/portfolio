Self-Custodial Digital Currency Wallet

MetaMask

MetaMask is a popular cryptocurrency wallet and gateway to decentralized apps on the Ethereum blockchain. Available as a browser extension and mobile app, it allows users to securely store, send, and swap Ethereum and ERC-20 tokens. With built-in Web3 integration, MetaMask makes it easy to interact with DeFi platforms, NFT marketplaces, and blockchain games. Its user-friendly interface and strong security features make it a top choice for crypto beginners and experts alike.

OpenSea is the world's largest NFT marketplace, where users can buy, sell, and discover digital collectibles, art, gaming items, and more. Web3 Journey

2020-Now
Going Further in Crypto
In 2020, I fully immersed myself in crypto - transforming from casual observer to active participant.

• Mastered Web3 Gaming - Went beyond Axie to explore emerging play-to-earn ecosystems, analyzing tokenomics and gameplay loops.

• Became an Airdrop Hunter - Developed strategies to identify promising projects early, participating in numerous campaigns (with mixed results).

• Leveled Up My Trading - Transitioned from HODLing to technical analysis, learning to read charts and manage risk more effectively (still working on risk management and proper entries and exits).

This period marked my shift from crypto-curious to crypto-committed - making mistakes, learing and adapting, scoring wins, and constantly expanding my skill set in this fast-moving space.
October 2024 - Current
UNIDOCCS Development and Testing
I'm currently developing an innovative decentralized documents platform, pushing the boundaries of how we create, share, and verify digital content. This work recently earned academic recognition when I successfully defended my research papers at the 1st CBA Student Research Colloquium on April 21, 2025, hosted by Baguio Central University. The project represents my vision for a more transparent, secure, and user-controlled approach to document management - combining my crypto experience with practical Web3 solutions.
February 2019 - 2021
Introduction to Web3
My first real dive into crypto wasn't through trading—it was through playing. I discovered Axie Infinity at its peak, back when "scholarships" were turning gamers worldwide into earners. Under a strict but skilled Korean manager, I leveled up fast, learning the meta, optimizing SLP grinding, and even ranking among the top scholars in our guild. The late night wake-up calls for energy resets and the thrill of cashing out AXS rewards felt like a crazy hybrid of gaming and a part-time job. But when my manager returned to Korea and the Axie economy started shifting, I cashed out my last earnings and moved on—with a permanent appreciation for how Web3 could turn play into real value.
2020
Deeper Dive Into Crypto
I dipped deeper into crypto during the 2020 lockdowns, back when everyone was stuck at home. I started buying small amounts of ETH and other altcoins, riding the 2021 bull run highs and suffering through 'I should have held longer' and 'I should have sold earlier'. As a top scholar under a strict Korean manager, I lived the play-to-earn life: waking up at 4AM to optimize energy, studying Axie metas like its a full-time job, and cashing out SLP and AXS while the economy boomed (then it crashed). It was my first taste of Web3's real-world utility.

Now? I'm still here, but wiser. NFTs taught me speculation, DeFi taught me risk, and Axie taught me that even "the future of gaming" needs reality checks. Web3 isn't just charts—it's communities, ownership, and sometimes harsh wake-up calls.
